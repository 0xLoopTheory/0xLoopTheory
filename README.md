# 0xLoopTheory

Cryptologist and cybersecurity researcher focused on post-quantum cryptography, zero-knowledge proof systems, and secure systems engineering.

## Research

- **Post-quantum ZK security:** analyzing how deployed ZK proof systems break under quantum threat models and what migration looks like at the protocol level
- **PQC transition analysis:** tracking NIST standardization, measuring real-world adoption, identifying where migration guidance falls short
- **Cryptographic inventory:** CBOM generation, dependency-level crypto scanning, readiness assessment methodology

## Current work

Building [Encryptorium](https://encryptorium.com), an independent applied cryptography research platform covering the post-quantum transition with original research, technical analysis, and open resources.
